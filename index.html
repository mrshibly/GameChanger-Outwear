// SYSTEM INTEGRATION

Smart System Workflow

Trace the operational steps from turning on the device to automated thermal comfort regulation.

Step 01

Wear & Power On

Active Components:

  • 3S Li-ion Battery
  • 3S 40A BMS
  • Power Switch
  • LM2596 Buck Converter
  • ESP32 DevKit

Process Description:

The high-capacity 3S Li-ion battery supplies raw power to the smart jacket. The BMS protects the cells against overcharge, while the LM2596 Buck Converter regulates voltage down to a stable 5V. Finally, the ESP32 DevKit microprocessor boots and initializes the control program.

// QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers about the jacket's thermochromic behaviors, charging, and general operations.

Thermochromic fabric changes color in response to temperature variations. Micro-encapsulated liquid crystals or leuco dyes embedded in the fabric fibers change their molecular shape at 30°C, shifting the light reflectivity from cold blue to a fiery orange/red, providing an instant visual readout of thermal activity.

Yes, the THI-THU V1 incorporates weather-resistant treated outer fabrics and features waterproof zippers. The electrical controller and battery compartments are fully sealed, making the jacket highly safe to wear in light rain or snow. (Note: Remove the battery before hand washing or heavy cleaning).

Battery backup depends on the active operating mode. In low heating mode, the 3S Li-ion battery lasts up to 6–8 hours. In high heating or full-zone mode, it lasts around 3–4 hours. Using the automatic comfort regulation setting conserves the cells, extending the runtime up to 10+ hours by cycling power dynamically.

Yes. The smart jacket features an integrated physical push button switch with LED indicators on the sleeve. You can toggle basic automatic climate mode or step through preset heating zones manually. The mobile app unlocks precision target setting, zone configurations, and live charts.

The jacket features a 3-zone smart heating system. You can configure power individually for the Left Chest, Right Chest, and Back heating pads, customizing comfort where you need it most.
